PMO Charter

A foundational document defining authority, governance, scope, and operating structure for PMO functions.

Purpose of the PMO Charter

The PMO Charter establishes the mandate, authority, and governance structure of the Program Management Office. It defines roles, responsibilities, decision rights, and the PMO’s relationship to leadership and stakeholders.

Key Components

  • PMO mission & objectives
  • Governance structure & decision rights
  • Roles & responsibilities
  • Reporting lines & escalation pathways
  • Performance expectations & KPIs

Why It Matters

A strong PMO Charter ensures clarity, accountability, and alignment across complex infrastructure programs.

Procurement SOP

Standardized procedures for transparent, compliant, and defensible procurement actions.

Purpose of the SOP

The Procurement SOP defines step-by-step procedures for planning, soliciting, evaluating, and awarding procurements in alignment with donor-funded and government requirements.

Core SOP Elements

  • Procurement planning
  • Solicitation development
  • Bid evaluation procedures
  • Award documentation
  • Contract administration

Why It Matters

SOPs ensure consistency, compliance, and defensibility across all procurement actions.
