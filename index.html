About Global Infrastructure Advisory

Delivering advisory, compliance, and mission‑critical support for donor‑funded infrastructure programs worldwide. Built on experience, driven by integrity, and committed to global impact.

Founder & Executive Leadership

Global Infrastructure Advisory (GIA) was founded by Kenneth Bennett, a federal acquisition and real property executive with a career spanning public service, defense contracting, infrastructure delivery, and mission support across the United States and global development environments.

Kenneth has served as Assistant Commissioner for the Office of Acquisition Management at the U.S. General Services Administration (GSA), overseeing national procurement strategy, policy, and governance for one of the largest public real property portfolios in the world. His leadership includes senior contracting roles supporting Walter Reed, SAIC, and major federal programs, as well as hands‑on experience in construction, electrical systems, and property management.

This combination of executive‑level governance and field‑level technical experience gives GIA a unique advantage: the ability to bridge policy, compliance, engineering, and delivery — ensuring donor‑funded programs achieve measurable, sustainable impact.

Kenneth holds a Master of Public Administration (MPA) and a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration, grounding GIA’s work in both operational discipline and mission‑driven public service.

Our Mission

To strengthen global infrastructure delivery by providing advisory, compliance, and operational support that empowers governments, donors, and implementing partners to deliver high‑quality, transparent, and resilient infrastructure programs.

Our Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of ethics, transparency, and accountability in every engagement.

Impact

Our work is measured by the long‑term benefits delivered to communities and partner nations.

Technical Excellence

We combine engineering, acquisition, and operational expertise to support complex infrastructure programs.

Service

Rooted in public service, we prioritize mission outcomes over commercial interests.

Resilience

We help partners build infrastructure that withstands environmental, economic, and operational challenges.

Global Partnership

We collaborate with donors, governments, and implementing partners to deliver shared outcomes.

Global Footprint

GIA supports programs across Sub‑Saharan Africa, North Africa & the Middle East, Asia‑Pacific, Latin America & the Caribbean, Europe & Central Asia, and North America.

Our work spans energy, water, transportation, digital infrastructure, health, education, and climate resilience — enabling governments and donors to deliver infrastructure that transforms lives.
