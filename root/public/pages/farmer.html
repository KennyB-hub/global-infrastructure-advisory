🧠 Seven‑of‑Nine — Livestock & Land Intelligence

Seven‑of‑Nine is the intelligence layer that helps farmers manage land, livestock, and boundaries with clarity and confidence. She is not here to control anything — she is here to explain what’s happening and help you make the best call.

Seven monitors the things that are easy to miss:

  • 🐄 Cattle drifting toward a road or boundary
  • 📡 A collar that hasn’t checked in
  • 🌱 Pastures needing rest or rotation
  • 🛰️ Fence lines that need moved 500 feet
  • 🌪️ Weather or disaster conditions that change the plan

Seven turns complex data into simple language:
“Your cattle are safe.”
“Pasture B is ready to graze.”
“Move the line 500 feet for fresh grass.”
“One animal hasn’t checked in for 32 minutes.”

Seven is built for farmers, families, and rural communities — a system that works in the hills, the hollers, and the back roads, not just the cities.
